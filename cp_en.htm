(Sample) Mayor Chang Inspects 2025 Taiwan Lantern Festival Trial Run, Invites Families to Celebrate the Lantern Festival Together

  • Release Unit:桃園文化局
  • Release Date:2025/03/13

On the morning of February 7, Taoyuan Mayor Chang San-Cheng inspected the “City Spotlight” and “Bright Horizons Theater” zones of the 2025 Taiwan Lantern Festival. Mayor Chang highlighted that this year’s festival, themed “Lights Playground,” features five major lantern zones: “City Spotlight,” “Bright Horizons Theater,” “Welcome to Chintan Park,” “Vibrant Light Hub,” and “Fortune’s Glow.” Each zone showcases Taoyuan’s rich multicultural heritage, integrating elements from new immigrant, Hakka, Minnan, and Indigenous cultures. He believes that the festival’s diverse and vibrant displays will leave a lasting impression on visitors. The Taiwan Lantern Festival has now entered its trial operation phase, and Mayor Chang welcomes everyone to visit Taoyuan and celebrate the Lantern Festival together.


Mayor Chang noted that Taoyuan is a diverse and family-friendly city where many young families choose to settle. While visiting the “City Spotlight” zone, he observed how it embodies Taoyuan’s spirit of cultural convergence. Meanwhile, the “Bright Horizons Theater,” located at the intersection of Linghang North Road Section 1 and Huanxi 1st Street, features a circus-themed “Bouncy Circus.” Using large-scale special lighting installations, this immersive and whimsical attraction is designed for families to enjoy together. Mayor Chang praised the dynamic and unique characteristics of each lantern zone and expressed confidence that the spectacular light performances of this year’s Taiwan Lantern Festival will captivate visitors and make their experience unforgettable.
