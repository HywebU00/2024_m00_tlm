About Us

On October 18, 2017, Cai Zhiyang, the former librarian of the Taoyuan City Cultural Affairs Bureau, sent a letter inviting four literary creators, Lin Wenyi, Lin Yangmin, Chen Xiamin, and Chen Mingpan, as well as the local mayors and library colleagues, to hold a “Taoyuan Literature Museum Preparatory Consultation Meeting” in the meeting room of Taoyuan Branch of Taoyuan Municipal Library, to formally announce that Taoyuan City would soon build a literary museum.This is a literary base designed to promote Taoyuan's literary writing and publishing, to showcase the lives of literary writers and their creative processes, to connect with local literature and history, to establish a platform for literary exchanges, to present Taoyuan's literary development, and to serve as an indicative cultural window.As the capital of the nation, Taoyuan's original intent in establishing the museum was to fulfill the main goal of a “literary museum,...
